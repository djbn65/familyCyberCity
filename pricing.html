Party Packages

Planning a party to be held here? We have multiple different options you can choose from to make your party the best without breaking the bank. Click on each package to view more details about it.

The Beginner's Package

Access to the private party room with access to all activities during the party's length. This package limits the amount of guests, and discounted food is included, as well.

The "Not-So-Virtual" Reality Package

Access to the private party room with unlimited, private acccess to all outdoor activities. This package limits the amount of guests, and discounted food is also included.

The Dream Experience Package

Access to the private party room with unlimited access to all activities during the party's length. There is no guest limit, but additional guests will raise the price. One meal is included for each guest, as well.

A Techie's Virtual Utopia Package

Private access to the whole building for as long as you reserved with an included meal for each guest. This is the ultimate party package for the ultimate tech junkie.
