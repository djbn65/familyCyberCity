About Us

Amenities

See what we have to offer here at Family Cyber City.

Free WiFi

Access our free WiFi from anywhere within our facilities, so you can share your favorite pictures at any time.

Signature Food

Every one of our food items has a signature name and style developed by the cooks here at Family Cyber City.

Smoke Free

We are a family friendly organization, and we prohibit all smoking within our facilities for the health of our customers.

Charging Stations

We offer multiple charging stations, including one near each activity and multiple others in our dining area.

Easy Location

Located in the outskirts of New York, we offer an exciting experience while staying away from the hustle and bustle of the city.

Newest Techs

We strive to provide a strong technology background, so we implement the latest technologies in our activities.

Everyone Welcome

Technology shouldn't only be for the younger generation. Our activities are fit to appeal to all ages.

Open Everyday

Experience your favorite activities any day of the week, so you never have to stop having fun here at Family Cyber City.

Hours

We are open everyday of the week with extended hours on the weekends for those late night parties.
